Design Styles

Choose from our diverse range of architectural styles, each crafted to reflect different regional traditions and personal preferences.

Traditional

Classic Indian temple architecture with intricate carvings, traditional motifs, and time-honored design elements.

Contemporary

Modern minimalist approach with clean lines, subtle details, and integration with contemporary home aesthetics.

Rajasthani

Ornate Rajasthani craftsmanship featuring detailed jali work, arches, and traditional Rajputana architectural elements.

South Indian

Dravidian temple architecture with gopuram-inspired designs, pillared halls, and traditional South Indian motifs.

Gujarati

Elegant Gujarati craftsmanship with delicate carvings, geometric patterns, and refined architectural details.

Fusion

Blend of traditional and modern elements, creating unique designs that honor heritage while embracing innovation.
