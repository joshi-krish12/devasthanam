Architectural Styles

Choose from our diverse range of temple architectures, each representing different regional traditions and spiritual aesthetics.

North Indian Style

Characterized by towering shikharas, intricate carvings, and ornate doorways. These temples feature the classic Nagara architectural style with detailed sculptures and geometric patterns.

  • Tall spire (Shikhara) design
  • Elaborate entrance portals
  • Detailed relief carvings
  • Traditional motifs and patterns

South Indian Style

Inspired by Dravidian architecture with pyramid-shaped towers (gopurams), pillared halls, and intricate stone work. Perfect for those seeking traditional South Indian temple aesthetics.

  • Pyramid-shaped gopuram
  • Pillared mandapa halls
  • Detailed stone sculptures
  • Traditional Dravidian elements

Contemporary Style

Modern interpretation of traditional temple architecture with clean lines, minimalist approach, and integration with contemporary home designs while maintaining spiritual essence.

  • Clean, modern lines
  • Minimalist design approach
  • Contemporary material usage
  • Space-efficient layouts
