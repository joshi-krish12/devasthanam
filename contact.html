Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a pooja room?

Typically, our pooja rooms take 4-8 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity of design and customization requirements. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide complete installation services with our expert team. Installation is included in all our packages and comes with warranty coverage.

Can you work with my interior designer?

Absolutely! We collaborate closely with interior designers and architects to ensure seamless integration with your overall home design.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide comprehensive warranty ranging from 1-5 years depending on the package. This covers craftsmanship, materials, and installation quality.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we serve clients globally through Devasthanam International. We handle all shipping, customs, and installation coordination for international projects.

Can I see samples before ordering?

Yes, you can visit our experience centre to see samples, or we can arrange for sample delivery for serious inquiries. Virtual tours are also available.
